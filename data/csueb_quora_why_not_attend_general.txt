Document Topic: General concerns and student opinions about attending CSU East Bay
School: CSU East Bay
Department: General university context

Content:
Quora commenters describe CSU East Bay as a diverse, practical, and accessible
university where many students are hardworking, financially responsible, first-generation,
international, or balancing school with jobs and family responsibilities. One adjunct professor
explains that some students may take longer than four years to graduate because of real-life pressures such as work,
financial issues, family emergencies, and caregiving responsibilities.

Former students describe positive experiences with campus involvement, professor connections,
and academic support. One commenter says their best education came from CSU East Bay, even after earning
degrees from other universities.

A former Computer Science student says they completed CS coursework and an M.S.
in Computer Science at CSU East Bay and were pleased with the faculty, classes, evening scheduling,
and program quality. They mention that the program may not have the prestige of schools like MIT,
but they felt well prepared professionally and believed employers in Silicon Valley generally recognized the quality of CSUEB graduates.
