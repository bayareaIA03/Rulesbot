Content:
Several Quora commenters describe CSU East Bay as a decent and underrated university,
especially for students who want an affordable, accredited school in the Bay Area.
One commenter says CSU East Bay is not as highly ranked or as famous as nearby schools such as Stanford,
UC Berkeley, Santa Clara University, or San Jose State University, but still describes it as a good option depending on a student's situation, budget, program, and expectations.

The comments mention several positives about CSU East Bay. These include the campus location in Hayward,
shuttle access from the Hayward BART station, housing options, affordability, evening classes for working
professionals, and a diverse student population. One commenter also mentions that the university has programs
such as Business Analytics, Engineering Management, Computer Science, Accounting, Public Administration, and Statistics.

The comments also mention limitations. One commenter says CSU East Bay is not as famous as other California universities
and that students should not expect the same name recognition as top-ranked schools. Another concern is that career fairs
and job placement may not be as strong as higher-ranked universities. The comments also say that professor quality can be
mixed, with some strong professors and some weaker ones.

Overall, the Quora discussion presents CSU East Bay as an affordable, safe, accredited, and underrated university
with some strong academic programs. The comments do not claim that CSU East Bay is ranked at the same level as Stanford or
UC Berkeley. Instead, they suggest that CSUEB may be a practical choice for students who value affordability,
Bay Area location, evening classes, and access to programs such as Computer Science, Business Analytics,
Engineering Management, and Statistics.
